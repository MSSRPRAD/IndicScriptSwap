sataya tatarasaya daśa davatatama hamalaya nama nagadharaja 
    paravapara tayanadha vagahaya sathata pathavaya va manadaṇaḍa 
    ya saravaśala parakalapaya vatasa mara sathata dagadhara dahadakaṣa 
    bhasavanata ratanana mahaṣadhaśa ca pathapadaṣaṭa dadahara dharatarama 
    nanataratanaparabhavasaya yasaya hama na sabhagayavalapa jatama 
    ka ha daṣa gaṇasanapata namajajatanada karaṇaṣava vaṅaka 
    yaśa capasaravabharamamaṇaḍanana sapadayatara śakharara babharata 
    balahakacachadavabhakataragama kalasadhayama va dhatamatatama 
    makhala sacarata ghanana cachayama dhasanagata naṣavaya 
    davajata vaṣaṭabhara śarayanata śaṅagaṇa yasayatapavanata sadadha 
    pada taṣarasaratadhatarakata yasamanana daṣaṭavapa hatadavapanama 
    vadanata maraga nakharanadharamakatara makataphala kasaraṇa karata 
    nayasatakaṣara dhatarasana yatara bharajatavaca kañajarabanadaśaṇa 
    varajanata vadayadharasanadaraṇama naṅagalakhakarayayapayagama 
    ya parayana kacakaranadharabhagana daramakhatathana samaraṇana 
    dagasayatama cachata kanaraṇa tanaparadayatavama vapaganatama 
    kapalakaṇaḍa karabhara vanata vaghaṭaṭatana saraladaramaṇama 
    yatara saratakaṣarataya parasata sanana ganadha sarabhakarata 
    vanacaraṇa vanatasakhana daragahatasaṅaganaṣakatabhasa 
    bhavanata yataraṣadhaya rajanayama talapara sarataparadapa 
    davajayataya ṅagalaparaṣaṇabhagana maraga śalabhatahama pa yatara 
    na daravahaśaraṇapayadhararata bhanadanata manada gatama śavamakhaya 
    davakarada rakaṣata ya gahasa lana dava bhatama vanadhakarama 
    kaṣadara pa nana śaraṇa parapanana mamatavama cacaśarasa satava 
    laṅagalavakaṣapavasarapaśabhara tasa tataśa canadaramaracagara 
    yasayarathayakata gararajaśabada karavanata valavayajanaśa camaraya 
    yataraśakakaṣapavalajajatana yadacachaya kaparaṣaṅagananama 
    daragahadavaravalamababamabasa tarasakaraṇaya jalada bhavanata 
    bhagarathanarajharasakaraṇa vaḍha maha kamapatadavadara 
    yada vayara navaṣaṭamaga karatara savayata bhananaśakhaṇaḍabaraha 
    sapataraṣahasatavacatavaśaṣaṇaya dha vavasavana paravaratamana 
    padamana yasayagarasararahaṇa parabadhayataya radhavamakhara mayakha 
    yajañaṅagayanatavama vakaṣaya yasaya sara dharataradharaṇakaṣama ca 
    parajapata kalapatayajañabhaga śaladhapataya savayama navataṣaṭhata 
    sa manasa marasakha pataṇa kanaya kalasaya sathataya sathatajaña 
    mana mananama pa mananayama tamanarapa vadhanapayama 
    kalakaramaṇatha taya paravatata savarapayagaya sarataparasaṅaga 
    manarama yavanama davahanataya garabha bhavada bhadhararajapatanaya 
    sata sa nagavadhapabhagaya manakama mabhanadhabadadhasakhayama 
    karadadha pa pakaṣacachada vataraśatarava vadanajaña kalaśakaṣatanama 
    thavamanana pata parayakata dakaṣasaya kanaya bhavaparavapatana 
    sata sata yagavasaṣaṭadaha ta janamana śalavadha parapada 
    sa bhadharaṇama dhapana tasaya samadhamatayama dapada bhavaya 
    samayakaparayagada parakaṣataya natava vatasahagaṇana sapata 
    parasananadaka pasavavakatavata śaṅakhasavanananatarapaṣapavaṣaṭa 
    śararaṇa sathavarajaṅagamana sakhaya tajajanamadana babhava 
    taya dahatara satara savatara sapharataparabhamaṇaḍalaya cakasa 
    vadarabhamara navamaghaśabadada dabhananaya ratanaśalakayava 
    dana dana sa paravaradhamana labadhadaya canadaramasava lakha 
    papaṣa lavaṇayamayana vaśaṣaña jayatasananataraṇava kalanataraṇa 
    ta paravatataya bhajanana namana banadhaparaya banadhajana jahava 
     mata matara tapasa naṣadadha paśacada makhaya samakha jagama 
    mahabhata pataravata pa daṣaṭasa tasamanana pataya na jagama tapatama 
    nanatapaṣapasaya madhara ha cata davaraphamala savaśaṣasaṅaga 
    parabhamahataya śakhayava dapasa taramaragayava taradavasaya maraga 
    sasakaravatayava gara manaṣa taya sa pataśa ca vabhaṣataśa ca 
    manadakanasakatavadakabha sa kanadaka kataramapatarakaśa ca 
    rama mahara madhayagata sakhana karaḍarasa naravaśatava balaya 
    ta hasamala śaradava gaṅaga mahaṣadha nakatama vatamabhasa 
    satharapadaśama padaśakala parapadara parakatanajanamavadaya 
    sabhata maṇaḍanama ṅagayaṣaṭara nasavakhaya karaṇa madasaya 
    kamasaya paṣapavayatarakatama satara balayata para satha vaya parapada 
    namalata talakayava catara sarayaśabhara bhananama varavanadama 
    babhava tasayaśa catarasaraśabha vapara vabhakata navayavanana 
    bhayananataṅagaṣaṭhanakhaparabhabhara nakaṣapaṇada ragama vadagaranata 
    jaharatasa tacacaraṇa pathavaya sathalaravanadaśarayama vayavasathama 
    sa rajahasara va sanataṅaga gataṣa lalañacatavakaramaṣa 
    vayanayata paratayapadaśalabadhara datasabhara naparasañajatana 
    vatatanaparava ca na catadaragha jaṅagha śabha saṣaṭavatasa tadaya 
    śaṣaṅaganaramaṇavadha vadhatara lavaṇaya tapadaya vasa yatana 
    naganadarahasatasa tavaca karakaśatavada kanataśatayata kadalavaśaṣa 
    labadhavapa laka paraṇaha rapa jatasa tadaravara pamanabahaya 
    tavata nanava namayaśabha kañacagaṇasathanama nanadataya 
    rapata yada garaśana paśacada nanayanarakamanayama ṅakama 
    tasaya paravaṣaṭa natanabharanadhara raraja tanava navalamaraja 
    navama takaramaya satatarasaya tanamakhalamadhayamaṇara varaca 
    madhayana sa vadavalaganamadhaya valataraya cara babhara bala 
    rahaṇaratha navayavanana kamasaya sapanama va parayakatama 
    nayanayama tapaḍayada tapalakaṣaya satanadavaya paṇaḍa tatha paravadadhama 
    madhaya yatha śayamamakhasaya tasaya maṇalasataranatarama paya labhayama 
    śaraṣapaṣapadhakasakamaraya baha tadayava ta ma vataraka 
    parajatanapa kata harasaya ya kaṇaṭhapaśa makaradhavajana 
    kaṇaṭhasaya tasaya satanabanadharasaya makatakalapasaya ca nasatalasaya 
    nayanayaśabhajananada babhava sadharaṇa bhaṣaṇabhaṣayabhava 
    canadara gata padamagaṇana na bhaṅakata padamaśarata canadaramasama bhakhayama 
    mamakha ta paratapadaya lala davasaśaraya paratama vapa lakaṣama 
    paṣapa paravalapahata yada sayana makataphala va saphaṭavadaramasathama 
    tata nakarayada vaśadasaya tasayasa tamaraṣaṭhaparayasataraca samatasaya 
    savaraṇa tasayama matasaratava parajalapatayama bhajatavaca 
    paya nayapaṣaṭa paratakalaśabada śaratara vatanatarara va taḍayamana 
    paravatanalatapalanaravaśaṣama dharavaparakaṣatama yatakaṣaya 
    taya gahata na magaṅaganabhayasa tata gahata na magaṅaganabha 
    tasaya śalakañajananaramatava kanatara bharavara natalakhayara ya 
    ta vakaṣaya lalacatarama naṅaga savacapasanadarayamada mamaca 
    lajaja taraśaca yada catasa sayada saśaya paravatarajapataraya 
    ta kaśapaśa parasamakaṣaya karayara valaparayatava śathala camaraya 
    saravapamadaravayasamacacayana yathaparadaśa vanavaśatana 
    sa naramata vaśavasaja parayatanada kasathasanadarayadadakaṣayava 
    ta narada kamacara kada cata kanaya kala parakaṣaya pata samapa 
    samadadaśakavadha bhavatara paramaṇa śarararadhahara harasaya 
    gara paragalabha pa vayasaya ta sayasa tasatha navatatanayavarabhalaṣa 
    ta kaśanara na ha manatarapatama rahanata tajasaya paraṇa havayama 
    yacatara na ha davadavama dara sata garahayata śaśaka 
    bhayarathanabhaṅagabhayana sadhara madhayasathayama ṣaṭa paya valamabata ratha 
    yadava parava janana śarara sa dakaṣaraṣata sadata sasaraja 
    tadaparabhataya va vamakatasaṅaga pata paśanama paragaraha bhata 
    sa katatavasasa tapasa yatatama gaṅagaparavahakaṣatadavadara 
    parasatha hamadarara maganabhaganadha ka cata kavaṇatakanarama dhayavasa 
    gaṇa namaraparasavavatasa bharajatavaca saparaśavatara dadhana 
    manaśalavacacharata naṣada śalayanadadhaṣa śalatalaṣa 
    taṣarasaghataśala kharagara samalalakhana darapakala kakadamana 
    daṣaṭa katha cada gavayara vavaganara saḍhasahadhavanara nananada 
    tataraganama dhaya samatasamadadha savama va maratayanatarama ṣaṭamarata 
    savaya vadhata tapasa phalanama kanapa kamana tapaśa cacara 
    naraghayama raghayaṇa tama daranatha savaragakasama racatama racayatava 
    radhanayasaya sakhasamata samadadaśa parayata tanajama 
    paratayarathabhatama pa ta samadha śaśaraṣamaṇa garaśa namana 
    vakarahata sata vakarayanata yaṣa na catasa ta va dhara 
    vacatabalapaṣapa vadasamaragadakaṣa nayamavadhajalana barahaṣa capanatara 
    garaśama pacacara paratayaha sa sakaśa nayamataparakhada tacacharaśacanadarapada
